For over a decade, we've preserved the traditional art of barbering while embracing modern techniques. Our founder, Marco DeVitti, trained in the historic barber shops of Rome, brings authentic European grooming traditions to every service.

What sets us apart is our commitment to the complete experience - from the first hot towel to the final precision detail. We use only premium products and maintain the highest standards of cleanliness and professionalism.

The Man Behind the Chair

Professional Barber & Style Architect

I'm Marco Vazquez, third-generation barber and craftsman. What began as sweeping floors in my grandfather's shop evolved into a lifelong passion for the art of traditional grooming.

Specializing in vintage styles and modern adaptations, I believe a great haircut is equal parts technical precision and personal connection. Every client receives my undivided attention from first consultation to final touch-up.
